August 8, 2020

The First Time I Noticed You ❤️

I still remember the first time I saw you…though you may not even remember it, because we never spoke that day. But something about you stayed in my mind.Then after a long time, we met again.This time, we talked… we laughed… and even took a picture together. And that’s when something changed.I don’t know what it was…but I felt like I wanted to be around you more.I loved your company. At that time, I was already in a relationship…but still, my heart kept pulling me towards you. I didn’t understand it back then…whether it was love at first sight…or just a feeling that I wanted someone like you in my life. All I knew was…something about you felt different. ❤️

August 16, 2022

When My Heart Finally Spoke ❤️

There was always something special about us… something I couldn’t quite explain at first.Somewhere between our conversations, our laughter, and the moments we shared… I fell for you.I tried to tell you many times, but you gently stopped me—afraid that love might change our friendship. But what I feel for you was never something temporary…It stayed, it grew, and it became a part of who I am. So today, I’m taking that step…Not to change what we have, but to make it even more beautiful.I choose you—today, tomorrow, and always.❤️

December 4, 2022

A Drive, A Dream, and You ❤️

I know what you might be thinking… we had already gone out together so many times before. But this day was different. This day was special to me. Not that the others weren’t — I have countless memories with you. Honestly, it’s hard to choose just one… maybe you understand 😄 I wanted to take you somewhere… just the two of us. So while you were on your trip, I learned how to drive a car… just for you. And when you came back, I finally took you on that long drive. That day… is something I will never forget. I had always dreamed of going on a long drive with the person I love the most. And Allah didn’t just fulfill that dream…He gave me you. ❤️

February 15, 2023

You Said Yes 💍

Finally… the day came the one I had been waiting for. And honestly… I still don’t have the right words for that moment. Because what I felt… can’t be explained, only felt. When you said yes… you didn’t just accept my proposal, you gave me a happiness I had never known before. In that moment, I felt like the luckiest person in the world. I had you… and suddenly, I needed nothing else. ❤️

May 13, 2023

The Day We Became One👰

Seeing you walk towards me… felt like time itself had stopped. In that moment, nothing else mattered — it was just you and me. You took my breath away in a way I will never be able to explain. And when I said “Alhumdulliah”… I meant it with every part of my heart and soul. That wasn’t just a promise for that day… it was a promise for every tomorrow. You are not just my love… you are my greatest adventure, my most beautiful dream come true. ❤️

May 18 2023

Exploring the World Together ✈️

Every place is more beautiful through your eyes. Our honeymoon was a dream — new places, new memories, and always your hand in mine. Adventure is always better with you.

Our Little Miracle 👶

The day you became a mother was the day I fell in love with you all over again. Watching you hold our baby for the first time — that image is forever etched in my heart.

Our story is not finished. I believe the best chapters are yet to come.

I love you more than yesterday, and I will love you more tomorrow.

Always and forever, yours.
— Your Husband 💕
